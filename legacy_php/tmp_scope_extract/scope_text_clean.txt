1. Main Users/Roles
Administrator
Overall system manager.
Will:
Manage users
Register members
Create dues/levies
Configure system settings
View all reports
Approve/void payments
Manage backups
Treasurer / Finance Officer
Handles money-related activities.
Will:
Record payments
Verify payments
Generate receipts
View financial reports
Track arrears
Reconcile bank/MoMo payments
Secretary
Handles member records.
Will:
Register and update members
View payment status
Generate member reports
Send notices
Auditor / Chairman
Read-only oversight role.
Can:
View reports
View audit logs
View collections
Check arrears
Export reports
Member
Optional portal user.
Will:
Login
View dues
View arrears
Make payment
Download receipts
View payment history or Statement
